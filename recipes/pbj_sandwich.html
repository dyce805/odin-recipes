Description

This is a simple recipe for a peanut butter and jelly sandiwch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A spoonfull of jelly
  • A spoonfull of peanut butter

Steps

  1. Lay the bread flat
  2. Spread the Jelly on one slice
  3. Spead the Peanut Bitter on the other slice
  4. Combine the two pieces and you're done!
